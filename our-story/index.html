Our Story

Packaging is remembered long after the purchase.

We create thoughtful packaging systems that help exceptional brands look, feel and become unforgettable.

Discover our story

The beginning

Every premium product deserves an equally considered first impression.

When customers invest in something beautifully designed, the experience should not begin only when they see the product.

It begins when the package arrives. It begins with the texture of the box, the way the ribbon is tied, the message inside the envelope and the anticipation created before the product is revealed.

Luxsome was created from the belief that packaging should carry the same level of intention as the product it protects.

We help brands transform individual packaging elements into one cohesive system—designed to communicate quality, strengthen brand identity and make every customer feel valued.

Why we exist

Great products should never be diminished by an ordinary presentation.

Too many remarkable African brands create beautiful products, yet present them in packaging that does not fully reflect their quality.

The craftsmanship may be exceptional. The fabric may be carefully selected. The details may have taken weeks to perfect.

Yet before a customer experiences any of that, the packaging has already formed an impression.

Luxsome exists to close the gap between the quality of the product and the quality of its presentation.

Our goal is to help African brands compete visually with global brands while preserving their own distinctive identity.

Our mission

To help fashion and lifestyle brands look as premium as the products they create.

We believe exceptional packaging should be accessible to growing brands without requiring them to order thousands of units.

Our philosophy

Packaging is not a product. It is a system.

Luxury is rarely created by one element in isolation.

A beautiful box can make an impression. But when that box is paired with branded tissue, a thoughtful card, a coordinated envelope, a refined tag and the right finishing details, it becomes part of a complete experience.

That is why we do not approach packaging as a collection of unrelated products.

We consider how every piece looks, feels and works with the others, ensuring that the entire unboxing experience communicates one clear and consistent brand story.

How we work

Craftsmanship

Luxury lives in the details people can see and feel.

Materials, structure and finishing all influence how packaging is perceived.

We carefully consider the weight of the board, the texture of the paper, the depth of an impression, the accuracy of a fold and the way light moves across a finished surface.

These details may appear small individually, but together they determine whether packaging feels ordinary or exceptional.

  • Foil stamping
  • Embossing
  • Debossing
  • Spot UV
  • Textured paper
  • Soft-touch lamination
  • Magnetic closures
  • Custom inserts
